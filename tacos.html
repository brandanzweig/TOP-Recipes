Carne Picada Tacos

What is Carne Picada Tacos??

Carne picada is a cross between carne asada and ground beef. These tacos are simple and easy. I top mine with cilantro, onions, a squeeze of fresh lime and salsa.

Ingridient List

  • 8 (7 inch) corn tortillas, warmed
  • 2 cups prepared carne picada
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion

Taco Directions

  1. Heat tortialls directly on a gas grill or in the microwave.
  2. Place 2 tablesppons carne picada on each tortilla.
  3. Top with onions, cilantro, and salsa.
